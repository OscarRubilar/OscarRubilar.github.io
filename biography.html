Oscar Rubilar

Conductor

Oscar is a Chilean orchestral conductor based in Basel, Switzerland.

He is a Postgraduate in Orchestral Conducting from the Royal Academy of Music (London, UK) and a Master in Arts at the Musik-Akademie der Stadt Basel (Switzerland), with experience as both: Orchestral player and Orchestral Conductor.

Oscar has collaborated with orchestras and ensambles in Chile, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and Kosovo. His repertoire includes all kinds of music from the Barroque to the Contemporary
